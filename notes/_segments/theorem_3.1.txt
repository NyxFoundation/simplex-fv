### Statement (line 637)
Theorem 3.1 (Consistency). Suppose that two sequences of transactions, denote LOG and LOG′,
are both output output in honest view. Then either LOG ⪯LOG′ or LOG′ ⪯LOG (with overwhelming
probability in λ).
